If you wish to delete your account and all associated data from My Notes, please follow these steps:

  1. Open the My Notes app and go to Settings
  2. Tap on Delete Account
  3. Confirm the deletion when prompted

Once deleted:

  • Your Firebase-authenticated account (email/password or other sign-in method) will be permanently removed

We do not retain any user data after account deletion.
